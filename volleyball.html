Volleyball

Volleyball has been one of my greatest teachers of discipline and leadership. From being initially cut to eventually becoming a Varsity Defensive Specialist and club player, I’ve learned the value of persistence and teamwork on and off the court.

I also founded Elevate Volleyball, a free training initiative that has helped 20+ beginners earn placements in top New England clubs — transforming local access to competitive sports through community-driven coaching.
