Competitive Robotics

As Captain and Lead Mechanical Designer for FIRST Tech Challenge Team 22312 “BrobSTEM”, I’ve led our team to rank among the world’s top 0.5% of 8,000+ teams globally. From designing record-breaking mechanisms to optimizing autonomous routines, I’ve built robots that combine mechanical innovation, software precision, and team creativity.

Our designs — including the world-record scoring robot "Brobert" — showcase our drive to push the boundaries of competitive engineering. Through CAD modeling, manufacturing, and iterative testing, I learned how design thinking, leadership, and failure can work together to create world-class results.
