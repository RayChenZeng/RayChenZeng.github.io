Harvard Research

My research at Harvard University explores the intersection of AI, robotics, and human biomechanics. I worked on building EMG-controlled robotic systems capable of mimicking natural muscle movement — combining neural signal analysis, control theory, and mechanical design. This project taught me the importance of persistence, collaboration, and curiosity in scientific innovation.
